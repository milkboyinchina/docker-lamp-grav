# General Application Privacy Policy

*Effective Date: July 23, 2026*

## 1. Executive Summary & Developer Commitment
This Privacy Policy applies to all mobile applications, software, and services published by the developer.

**Our Core Privacy Promise**:  
> **No personal data is collected, stored, tracked, sold, or processed directly by the developer.**

We respect your personal privacy. As an independent developer, we do not build user profiles, collect personal information (such as your name, email address, phone number, location, or contacts), or operate private tracking servers.

---

## 2. Third-Party Services (Google Services)
While **the developer collects NO personal data directly**, our applications may rely on system frameworks and services provided by **Google LLC** ("Google") to function properly on Android and mobile ecosystems.

These third-party Google services may automatically collect non-personally identifiable diagnostic, technical, or telemetry data directly from your device.

### Google Services That May Collect Information:
1. **Google Play Services**: Provides core system APIs, authentication, security updates, and essential framework functionality.
2. **Google Play Analytics & Crash Reporting**: Automated technical crash logs, stack traces, and device hardware specifications (e.g., OS version, device model, memory state) to assist in fixing stability bugs.
3. **Google Mobile Ads / AdMob** *(if applicable)*: Non-personalized advertising identifiers or contextual signals governed by Google's ad policies.

### Data Types That May Be Processed by Google:
- **Device Identifiers**: Advertising ID, Android ID, or temporary app instance IDs.
- **System Performance & Diagnostic Data**: Crash logs, performance statistics, and app launch times.
- **Technical Device Information**: Device model, operating system version, screen resolution, and mobile network carrier.

---

## 3. How Google Uses This Information
Any data collected by Google through embedded SDKs or system services is processed independently in accordance with **[Google’s Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)** and **[Google Play Services Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites)**.

Google uses this information to:
- Ensure app stability, compatibility, and bug resolution.
- Prevent fraud, abuse, and security threats on Android devices.
- Deliver system services, updates, and framework APIs.

---

## 4. User Rights & Data Control
You remain in full control of your device data:
- **Advertising ID Reset**: You can reset or opt-out of personalized ads at any time via your Android settings: `Settings > Google > Ads > Reset advertising ID` or `Delete advertising ID`.
- **App Permissions**: You can review and revoke app permissions at any time under `Settings > Apps`.
